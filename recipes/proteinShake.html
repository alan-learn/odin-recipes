Protein Shake Recipe

This is bulk winnie the pooh favorite protein shake's receipe!

Chocolate Banana Peanut Butter Shake Ingredients

  • 6 ice cubes
  • 1 banana
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 scoop chocolate-flavored protein powder

How to Make Protein Shake

  1. Blend all the ingredients together in a blender until smooth.
  2. Wala! Enjoy the protein shake.
