Lasagna Recipe

This is garfield favorite lasagna's recipe!

Lasagna Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Egg

How to Make Lasagna

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

How to Layer Lasagna

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan
