Jalapeno Poppers Recipe

This is wade in elemental favorite jalapeno poppers' recipe!

Jalapeno Poppers Ingredients

  • 12 ounces cream cheese
  • 8 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon bacon bites
  • 12 ounces jalapeno peppers, seeded and halved
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 2 quarts oil for frying

How to Make Hot Jalapeno Poppers

  1. Mix cream cheese, Cheddar cheese and bacon bits together.
  2. Spoon the mixture into jalapeno halves.
  3. Put milk, flour, and bread crumbs in separate bowls.
  4. Dip stuffed jalapenos in milk, then in flour.
  5. Put coated jalapenos to a plate, and let dry for 10 minutes
  6. Dip jalapeno in milk once more, then roll through the bread crumbs.
  7. Heat oil to 365 degrees F in a medium skillet.
  8. Deep-fry jalapenos until golden brown 2 to 3 minutes.
